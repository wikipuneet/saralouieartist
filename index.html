The Artist

About Sara

Sara Louie is a UK-based fine line and botanical tattoo artist with over eight years of experience transforming ideas into permanent art. Based in her private studio, she specialises in delicate linework, intricate florals, and minimalist designs that honour the natural beauty of the human form.

Her approach is deeply collaborative — every session begins with listening. Sara believes that a tattoo should feel like an extension of who you are, not just something placed upon you. Her meticulous attention to detail and gentle technique have earned her a devoted following across the UK.

"I don't just draw on skin — I listen to stories, then translate them into something that will live with you forever."

When she's not tattooing, Sara can be found sketching botanical studies, exploring vintage tattoo archives, or wandering through London's flower markets for inspiration.

— Sara Louie
How It Works

The Process

01

Consultation

We discuss your vision, placement, size, and style. Bring reference images and let's explore ideas together — no pressure, just creativity.

02

Design

Sara creates a custom design tailored to your body and story. You'll receive a digital preview to review and refine before your session.

03

Tattoo Session

Relax in a calm, private studio environment. Sara works with precision and care, ensuring every line is placed with intention.

04

Aftercare

You'll receive detailed aftercare instructions and ongoing support. Sara checks in to ensure your tattoo heals beautifully.
